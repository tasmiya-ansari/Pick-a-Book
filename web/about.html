About

Pick-a-Book | Your all in one Bookstore

INTRODUCTION


Pick-A-Book is an online web application where the customer can purchase books online. In which the customer can Select a book by its title or author, later can add to the shopping cart and finally purchase the books.

Vision


  1. Customers can get their book delivered instead of actually going and buying the book. They can make payment online itself.
  2. Managing of inventory in the shop for shopkeeper becomes easier as customers are not visiting and ordering online.
  3. This system saves both time and travelling cost of customers.
  4. User can get to know different kinds of books that they were unaware of by just searching in the system using keywords.

Goals


Our Goal is to provide quality but affordable books for education, entertainment, self-development and self-fulfillment; to all when the need arises by: Providing a wide range of books to satisfy our clients. Exceeding our customers' expectation in their book requirements. Making our books accessible in the market.
